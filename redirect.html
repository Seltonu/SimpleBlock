How to use NoScroll

The seed of every habit is a single, tiny decision.

Your results are the product of either personal focus or personal distractions. The choice is yours.

Discipline is the bridge between goals and accomplishments.

Concentrate all your thoughts upon the work at hand. The sun's rays do not burn until brought to a focus.

NoScroll was created to help you stop endless scrolling.
How to Use

The following ruleset will block the homepage of Facebook, so you don't spend your time infinitely scrolling. Still want to keep in touch with friends? Set it to redirect to your messages!

Want to block a whole website?
Add the wildcard * at the end of your block rule so nothing gets through!

Block rules are intentionally flexible so you're in control. Quickly enable/disable your rules so you can decide when it's time to focus. We could make it harder to disable, but then you're not the one in control of your habits.
